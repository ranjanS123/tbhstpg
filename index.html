About Us

Once upon a time,
There were thousands and thousands of young parents who had no idea of what their child should watch or rather what their child is watching !!
They started showing their children what was popular but not necessarily what was appropriate.
These were dark times.
Even if parents wanted to make their child's screen time meaningful, where would they find such content?
Fortunately, times changed and a new platform came into being.
A platform that curates content for kids, so that parents don't have to.
A platform that sources original content from around the world, so that parents don't have to.
A platform that is a perfect mix of Entertainment and Learning.
And we're extremely happy to be associated with this endeavour !!
